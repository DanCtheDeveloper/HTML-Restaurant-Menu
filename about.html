About Us

Origin Story

Our story began by service San Diego locals the freshest Thai food in town from our Market street location, right downtown San Diego. Being downtown, we are able to be close to the working public, feeding all the hungry workers in our thriving downtown San Diego economy. From the office workers, to the construction workers, fireman, or security worker - everyone just loves our Thai food! So much that hose workers demanded we build new locations closer to their homes, and now their families love our food too! Try us today, we know you'll fall in love with us too!
